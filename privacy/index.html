Privacy Policy

Wordmere

A cozy word puzzle · Last updated: 18 June 2026

The short version: Wordmere is a single-player word puzzle that runs entirely on your device. No account is required, and we do not collect personal information that identifies you. The game works offline, and your progress and settings stay on your device.

Information we handle

Stored only on your device

  • Game progress — your level, coins, found words and daily-puzzle history, kept in local storage on your device (AsyncStorage) and never uploaded.
  • Settings — your sound, music and haptics preferences, stored locally.

Purchases

  • Optional in-app purchases (coin packs) are processed by the Apple App Store. We never receive or store your payment details — Apple handles the transaction and shares only confirmation that a purchase succeeded. Purchase validation is handled via RevenueCat. These requests are anonymous and contain no personal data.

Advertising & analytics

  • We do not show ads, and we do not use third-party analytics or tracking. If this ever changes in a future version, we will update this policy and disclose any provider before that version ships.

Children’s privacy

The game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data sharing

We do not sell your information. The only third parties involved are Apple and RevenueCat, strictly to process and validate in-app purchases. No gameplay or personal data is shared.

Your choices

  • Gameplay and settings data stay on your device; clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling removes them.
  • You can manage purchases in your Apple ID account settings.

Data retention

Locally-stored data persists until you clear it or uninstall the app. We do not maintain user accounts or server-side profiles.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change. Material changes will be noted in-app or on this page.
